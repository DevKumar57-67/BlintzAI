What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence is a paradigm of computing that aims to mimic human intelligence, enabling machines to learn from experience, adapt to new inputs, and perform tasks that traditionally require human intervention or AI is the ambition to create systems that can understand, learn, and apply knowledge, thereby extending human capabilities and automating routine tasks.

Ethical Use of Artificial Intelligence


◍Bias:AI systems learn from the data they are fed, and if this data contains biases, the AI will likely replicate or even exacerbate these biases.

◍Misuse: The potential misuse of AI technologies is a pressing concern. For instance, deepfakes, which employ AI to create realistic-looking video footage, can be used to spread misinformation or engage in malicious impersonation.

◍Data Privacy: AI systems often require vast amounts of data to function optimally, which raises serious data privacy concerns.

◍Elimination of Jobs : The automation of routine tasks by AI might lead to job displacement. While AI can create new job opportunities, the transition can be challenging, and there's a concern about the jobs that are lost in the process.
